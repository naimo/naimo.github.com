My PhD and post-doctoral research at the Massachussetts Institute of Technology was carried out in the Magnetic Materials and Devices Group, led by Prof. Caroline Ross in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering. The group researches new materials, device architectures and bottom-up fabrication methods for memory, logic and photonic devices.
